## Dissertation

**Title:** Semantically-Aware Generation of Vector Scene Sketches

The dissertation focused on improving scene-level sketch generation by enhancing semantic understanding in vector-based representations. The work involved modifying existing research architectures, integrating specialised encoders, and evaluating results using sketch-based image retrieval techniques.
